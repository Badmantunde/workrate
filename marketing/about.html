About

We built WorkRate to make trust measurable.

WorkRate started as a frustration shared by freelancers and founders: traditional time tracking tools either feel like surveillance or leave too much room for doubt. We wanted a third option that respected privacy and still gave clients the confidence to approve invoices in seconds.

Instead of screenshots or vague timesheets, WorkRate tracks verified time at the browser-tab level and turns that into a simple proof report. Freelancers get fair credit for focused work, and clients get a clear view of what happened during the hours they are paying for.

Who we serve

WorkRate is built for independent professionals, small agencies, and remote teams that bill by the hour or on retainers. If your business depends on long-term client relationships, transparent reporting is not a nice-to-have; it is a competitive advantage.

  • Designers and developers who want cleaner client conversations about scope and effort
  • Consultants and strategists who work across many tools in the browser all day
  • Studios and agencies who need a consistent, privacy-conscious way to show value

What we believe

Work should be judged on outcomes and honest signals, not on invasive monitoring. That is why we only track domains, never page content, and why your proof reports are built around verified focus time instead of screenshots or keystrokes.
