Data & security

Last updated: Feb 2026

WorkRate is built to be a trusted layer between you and your clients. That trust depends on how we handle your data and how we secure the platform.

Data minimization

WorkRate collects only the information needed to provide verified time tracking:

  • Account details such as name, email, and password hash.
  • Subscription information for paid plans.
  • Verified time tracking events focused on domains and timestamps.

What the extension sees

The browser extension works at the domain level. It is not designed to inspect or store the contents of the pages you visit. Its job is to know whether you are on a registered project tab, off-task, or idle.

Storage and security practices

WorkRate uses industry-standard practices to protect your data, including:

  • Using encrypted connections (HTTPS) between your browser, the extension, and our servers.
  • Storing authentication secrets and credentials securely.
  • Restricting access to production systems to authorized personnel.

Your responsibilities

Security is shared. You are responsible for using strong passwords, keeping your devices secure, and managing who has access to your WorkRate account and generated proof links.

Reporting a security issue

If you believe you have found a vulnerability or security issue in WorkRate, email us at security@workrate.io. Please include enough detail for us to reproduce and investigate the problem. We appreciate responsible disclosure.
