Privacy policy

Last updated: Feb 2026

WorkRate is designed around the principle of privacy by design. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

What we collect when you use WorkRate

When you create and use a WorkRate account, we may collect:

  • Account information such as your name, email address, and password hash.
  • Subscription and billing details if you upgrade to a paid plan.
  • Verified time tracking data from the extension, including domains and timestamps.
  • Basic device and usage data to keep the service secure and reliable.

What the extension records

The WorkRate browser extension is built to minimize the data it collects:

  • It records domain names only (for example, figma.com or github.com).
  • It does not store full URLs, page content, keystrokes, or screenshots.
  • It tracks timestamps that indicate when you were on a registered project domain, off-task, or idle.

How we use your data

We use your data to:

  • Provide the WorkRate service, including your dashboard and proof reports.
  • Help you understand your own time and improve client reporting.
  • Maintain and improve the reliability, security, and performance of WorkRate.
  • Communicate with you about updates, billing, and important product changes.

How we share data

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with trusted service providers who help us operate WorkRate (for example, hosting and analytics), under agreements that require them to protect your data.

Your choices

  • You can update your account information from the WorkRate dashboard.
  • You can uninstall the browser extension at any time.
  • You can contact us to request access to or deletion of your account data, subject to legal obligations.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, email us at privacy@workrate.io.
